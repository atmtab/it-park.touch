IT VISA is a visa for foreign IT specialists and their families. It grants the right to live and work in Uzbekistan, as well as access to benefits and support programs for IT professionals.

Who can apply
Foreign IT specialists, founders and key employees of IT companies
What it provides
The right to reside and work, and access to incentives and support programs
How to apply
Prepare the documents and submit your application online
